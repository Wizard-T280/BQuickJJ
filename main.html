Our Programs

From first-timers to competitors — every roll starts somewhere.

01 🥋
Adult BJJ
Ages 16+ · All Levels

Our flagship program covering fundamentals through advanced technique. Gi and no-gi options available. Whether you're day one or a seasoned blue belt — you belong on the mat.

02 ⭐
Kids BJJ
Ages 5–15 · Beginner Welcome

Build confidence, discipline, and body awareness in a safe, fun environment. Our kids program teaches real self-defense while building character and respect on and off the mat.

03 🔥
No-Gi / Submission Wrestling
All Levels · High Intensity

Rash guards only. Fast-paced, submission-focused grappling with a heavy emphasis on wrestling, leg locks, and modern BJJ. Perfect cross-training for all combat sports.

Open Mat
Saturdays · Members Free

Put the work in, then roll. Open mat is community — a chance to drill, spar, and grow with training partners of all levels. Visitors always welcome.

About BQuick JJ

Built on the Gulf

"The water teaches you to stay calm under pressure. The mat teaches you the same thing."

BQuick JJ was founded right here in Palmetto, FL — a community gym built by grapplers, for grapplers. We're not a franchise, not a McDojo. We're your neighbors who happen to love jiu-jitsu.

Southwest Florida has its own pace — laid back until it isn't. That's exactly how we train. Technique-first, ego-last, and when competition season hits, we show up with fire. Our students have competed and medaled at local, state, and national levels.

  • Structured curriculum from white to black belt
  • Certified, competition-tested instructors
  • Welcoming community — no egos tolerated
  • Climate-controlled facility (yes, it matters in Florida)
  • Flexible membership options for families & individuals
