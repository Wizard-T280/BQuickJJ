Our Philosophy

Beyond Sport Jiu-Jitsu

At BQuickJJ, we honor the roots of Jiu-Jitsu as a martial art for self-defense. While competition builds character and tests skill, our foundation is practical, real-world application.

As a combat veteran, Coach Quick understands what works under pressure. Every technique taught is pressure-tested and grounded in reality — whether you're a 4-year-old building confidence, a hobbyist getting in shape, or a professional fighter preparing for the cage.

The 12-month cyclical Fundamentals Curriculum ensures every student builds a complete foundation. This isn't a random collection of techniques — it's a structured program designed to take you from beginner to competent grappler, with the option to progress into advanced training.

Adult Jiu-Jitsu Fundamentals

Ages 13+ • All Levels

The first 12 months follow a structured Fundamentals Curriculum covering Jiu-Jitsu, Wrestling, and Self-Defense. This is a 12-month cyclical program for beginners, but all ranks are welcome.

After completing 12 months, students can comprehend basic Jiu-Jitsu movements, handle an opponent throwing strikes, and progress to advanced classes.
